Our Mission
"The personal care industry runs on skill, trust, and relationships — not spreadsheets and sticky notes."

Personal care professionals are among the most hardworking, talented people in any economy. A great barber, a gifted nail tech, a skilled esthetician — they build real relationships with the people they serve. But the business side? That's where things fall apart.

BookedOut exists to change that. We're building a platform that handles the operational weight of running a personal care business — so you can focus entirely on your craft and your clients.

Whether you're a solo independent or an owner managing an entire shop of chair renters, BookedOut gives you the tools, the automation, and the insights to grow — without the complexity.
